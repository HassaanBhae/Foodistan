Biryani

Description:

Biryani is a delicious and aromatic dish that hails from the Mughal empire in the Indian subcontinent.

It is a rice pilaf cooked with various spices, meat or fish, and vegetables.

Ingredients:

  • Oil
  • Onion and Garlic
  • Bone-in, cut up, skinless chicken
  • Ginger
  • Whole Spices
  • Tomatoes
  • Yogurt
  • Rice
  • Dried Plums (Alu Bukhara)

Steps:

  1. Prepare the chicken curry.
  2. Prepare the rice.
  3. Bring it all together for a final steam (‘dum‘).
  4. Make Biryani masala.
  5. Marinate the chicken in spices and yogurt.
  6. Layering the Chicken Biryani.
